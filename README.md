_____________________ArborNet: Ecological Connectivity Simulator________________________


ArborNet is a spatial-analysis dashboard designed to visualize, simulate, and heal urban canopy fragmentation. By mapping real-world satellite imagery into navigable ecological graphs, ArborNet helps city planners and conservationists protect local wildlife (like macaques) from the dangers of urban infrastructure expansion.

Features : 

Realtime Canopy Extraction: Uses YOLO-World and OpenCV contour mapping to identify dense tree clusters and translate them into a mathematical graph.
Habitat Vulnerability Simulation: Simulates the ecological impact of urban infrastructure (e.g., clearing trees for a power line) and tracks the immediate fragmentation of the habitat in real-time.
Algorithmic Mitigation (Canopy Bridges): Automatically pathfinds and overlays the most cost-effective wildlife overpasses (neon pink corridors) directly onto the satellite image to stitch  isolated ecosystems back togetherr.
